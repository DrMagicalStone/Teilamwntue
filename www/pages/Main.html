I am Teilamwntue

Construction

For a example

Look at the word "Teilamwntue", it seems that the word is constructed with some meaningful sections.

Teilamwntue = Tei + la + mon + tue

What does it means?

Tei = something sacred

la = high

mwn = meadow or a place with grass and flowers

tue = refer to a language

The language practical

Actually, Teilamwntue uses sections to make words, almost all the words can be divided into some parts, the words that come from other language not included.
